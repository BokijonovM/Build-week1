Maher Zain ( ماهر زين) is a Muslim Swedish R&B singer and songwriter, born 16 July 1981 in Tripoli,
Lebanon. He released his debut album Thank You Allah, an internationally successful album with strong
Muslim religious influences, in 2009. He released his follow-up album
Forgive Me in 2012, and has released his third album entitled "One” in 2016.
